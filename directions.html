Driving directions from Dundee city centre to Guardswell Farm
Download these directions


For Sat Nav users the Guardswell postcode is PH14 9QZ

Instructions

As a backup these are instructions on how to get there.

  • From the city centre, follow the signs for Perth A85
  • On the A85 after approx. 4.5 ml (7 km) you arrive at a roundabout. Head for Perth A90.
  • After approx. 4.5 ml (7 km) on the A90 (ignoring an earlier brown sign for Inchture) exit at a green sign reading:
    Inchture
    Abernyte
    Kinnaird
    B953
  • Turn right at the next roundabout following the sign reading:
    Abernyte
    Kinnaird
    B953
  • You cross back over the A90 then turn right at the next roundabout, following Kinnaird.
  • Shortly after this turn left at the next junction, again following Kinnaird.
  • Stay on this road and eventually you’ll drive through a small place named Ballindean. Keep going.
  • After another 1 mile (1.6 km) approx. you come to a crossroads. Turn right here.
  • Drive up the hill until a junction. Ignore the Kinnaird Village sign. Take the sharp bend to the right, following the Abernyte sign.
  • Continue on the road for approx. 0.5 ml (0.85 km) and you come to a sharp left-hand bend. Immediately after the bend the entrance to Guardswell is on the left.
  • Continue up the main driveway and you’ll find ample parking behind the main buildings.
