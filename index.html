The Celebration

The celebration will be at Guardswell Farm –

It takes 20 minutes by road to travel between St Andrew’s Cathedral and Guardswell Farm. Directions can be found here.

Planes, Trains and Automobiles

How to get to Guardswell Farm


Nearest airports

Dundee airport:
Fly direct from London City Airport on Loganair.

Glasgow airport:
Fly direct from London Stansted, Gatwick or Heathrow.
Distance by car from Glasgow airport to Dundee: 150km (approx 1 hr 40 mins)

Edinburgh airport:
Best option for guests from Sweden, Served by Norwegian & SAS. Many flight options from London & other international destinations.
Distance by car from Edinburgh airport to Dundee: 98km (approx 1 hr 10 mins)


Trains & buses

There are trains to Dundee and Perth from London King’s Cross.
There are regular train and bus services to Perth and Dundee from both Edinburgh and Glasgow.
